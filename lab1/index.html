The Exciting World of Cricket

What is Cricket?

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players on a field. It is one of the most popular sports, particularly in countries like India, England, and Australia.

Popular Cricket Formats

Cricket is played in several different formats, each with its own set of rules. The three main formats are:

  • Test Cricket
  • One-Day Internationals (ODIs)
  • T20 Internationals
